Q & A

The wedding is at Casale Doria Pamphilj, a beautifully restored organic farm and granary in the Roman countryside — just 25 km from central Rome and 10 minutes from the Mediterranean coast. The estate features a main garden, an antique wine cellar ("Cantinone"), a courtyard bar, and a swimming pool.
The venue is 30 minutes from both Rome airports — Fiumicino (FCO) and Ciampino (CIA). The venue can help pre-arrange minivans or coaches for groups. We recommend flying into Rome and arranging a transfer. We will share more transportation details closer to the date.
On-site accommodation is €75 per person per night, including a full buffet breakfast each morning. Single room supplement is €20/night. Children under 12 stay free when sharing their parents' room. The estate has 23 rooms and 7 apartments accommodating up to 80 guests.
We are covering all three main event meals — the Day 1 Welcome BBQ, the full wedding day reception and multi-course dinner, and the Day 3 Farewell Pizza Party. Unlimited wine, beer, and prosecco are included during all three events. Buffet breakfast is included with your room each morning.
Guests are responsible for their own flights, accommodation (€75/person/night), and transfers. Lunches are available to purchase daily at the Bar Emporio. Spirits and cocktails outside of event times are available at the bar on consumption. Optional excursions are at your own cost.
We recommend budgeting approximately €150–€250 per person for the full weekend. This comfortably covers 3 nights accommodation, airport transfers, a few lunches, and any extra drinks or excursions you choose to enjoy.
Wedding Day: Formal attire. The venue has a romantic, rustic-chic atmosphere so gowns, suits, and cocktail dresses are all welcome.

Day 1 BBQ & Day 3 Pizza Party: Smart-casual. Think sundresses, linen trousers, and relaxed evening wear. Don't forget comfortable shoes if you plan to explore the countryside.
Absolutely. Children ages 0–3 eat free at all events. Ages 4–11 have a special children's menu (€25–30). Ages 12–17 are charged at 50% of the adult rate. Kids under 12 stay free sharing parents' room. The estate also has an outdoor pool, playground, and a games room.
Day 3 is all about relaxing before the evening pizza party. On-site you have the pool, games room, and organic farm to explore. Nearby options include Singita Beach (20 min), Lake Bracciano & Castle Odescalchi (30 min), a day trip to Rome (25 km), or a pizza-making class or wine & cheese tasting right on the estate.

Getting There

Travel

Getting Here

Casale Doria Pamphilj is just 25 km from central Rome — 30 minutes from both Rome airports (FCO & CIA). The venue can pre-arrange minivans or coaches from the airport for groups.

Stay On-Site

23 rooms + 7 apartments accommodating up to 80 guests. €75/person/night including buffet breakfast (single supplement €20/night). Kids under 12 stay free sharing parents' room.

The Weekend

Day 1 — Arrive & Welcome BBQ at 7:30 PM
Day 2 — The Wedding ceremony, dinner & after-party in the antique "Cantinone" cellar
Day 3 — Relax + Farewell Pizza Party at 7:00 PM

What's Included

All three main event meals, unlimited wine, beer & prosecco during events, and daily buffet breakfast. Lunch, spirits, and extra drinks are available to purchase at the Bar Emporio.

Budget Guidance

Budget approx. €150–€250/person for the full weekend. This covers accommodation, transfers, a few lunches, and any extra drinks or excursions you choose.

Explore Rome

Things To Do

Singita Beach

A classy, relaxed Mediterranean beach just 20 minutes from the venue. Perfect for the morning of Day 3.

Lake Bracciano

Historic lake town and the stunning Castle Odescalchi — only 30 minutes away. Great for a morning excursion.

Rome

The Eternal City is just 25 km away. Colosseum, Vatican, Trevi Fountain, luxury shopping on Via Condotti — all easily arranged.

Pizza Making Class

Book a hands-on pizza making course or a wine & cheese tasting right on the estate (~€50/person).

On-Site Amenities

Outdoor pool, kids' playground, and a games room with pool table, table tennis & table football — all included.

Family Activities

Acquafelix waterpark (30 min) or Zoomarine theme park (60 min) — great for families with kids.
